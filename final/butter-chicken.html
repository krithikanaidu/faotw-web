Butter Chicken

Butter Chicken, also known as Murgh Makhani, is a popular Indian dish known for its rich, creamy tomato-based sauce. This dish features tender chicken pieces marinated in yogurt and spices, then cooked in a velvety sauce made with butter, cream, and aromatic spices. It's a perfect balance of flavors that will leave you craving more.

prep time

20 min.

cook time

30 min.

serving

4 servings

Tags : non-vegetarian curry chicken main course

instructions

step 1

Marinate the chicken pieces in yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric, red chili powder, and garam masala for at least 2 hours or overnight.

step 2

Heat oil in a pan and cook the marinated chicken until it's lightly browned. Remove and set aside.

step 3

In the same pan, melt butter and add chopped onions, ginger, garlic, and spices. Cook until onions are soft.

step 4

Add tomato puree and cook until oil separates. Add cream and the cooked chicken. Simmer for 10-15 minutes.

ingredients

500g boneless chicken

1 cup yogurt

2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste

1 tsp turmeric powder

1 tsp red chili powder

1 tsp garam masala

2 tbsp butter

1 cup tomato puree

1/2 cup fresh cream

Salt to taste

tools

Large Mixing Bowl

Heavy Bottom Pan

Measuring Spoons

Wooden Spoon
