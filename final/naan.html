Garlic Naan

Garlic Naan is a soft, fluffy Indian flatbread that's traditionally cooked in a tandoor oven. This version can be made on a stovetop and is brushed with garlic butter for extra flavor. Perfect for scooping up curries or enjoying on its own, this bread is a staple in Indian cuisine.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

4 servings

Tags : vegetarian bread side dish indian bread

instructions

step 1

Mix flour, yogurt, baking powder, salt, and sugar in a bowl. Add water gradually to form a soft dough. Knead for 5-7 minutes until smooth.

step 2

Cover the dough and let it rest for 2 hours. Meanwhile, prepare garlic butter by mixing melted butter with minced garlic and chopped coriander.

step 3

Divide the dough into equal portions and roll each into an oval shape. Heat a tawa or griddle over medium heat.

step 4

Cook the naan on the tawa until bubbles appear. Flip and cook the other side. Brush with garlic butter and serve hot.

ingredients

2 cups all-purpose flour

1/2 cup yogurt

1 tsp baking powder

1/2 tsp salt

1 tsp sugar

Water as needed

2 tbsp butter, melted

2 cloves garlic, minced

1 tbsp fresh coriander, chopped

tools

Mixing Bowl

Rolling Pin

Tawa or Griddle

Pastry Brush
