Chana Masala

Chana Masala is a popular North Indian dish made with chickpeas cooked in a spicy and tangy tomato-based gravy. This vegetarian dish is packed with protein and flavor, making it a perfect main course. The combination of aromatic spices and tender chickpeas creates a hearty and satisfying meal.

prep time

15 min.

cook time

25 min.

serving

4 servings

Tags : vegetarian curry chickpeas main course

instructions

step 1

Soak chickpeas overnight or use canned chickpeas. If using dried chickpeas, pressure cook until tender.

step 2

Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds, chopped onions, ginger, and garlic. Saut√© until onions turn golden brown.

step 3

Add tomato puree and spices (turmeric, coriander powder, cumin powder, garam masala, and red chili powder). Cook until oil separates.

step 4

Add the cooked chickpeas and water. Simmer for 10-15 minutes. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot.

ingredients

2 cups cooked chickpeas

2 tbsp oil

1 tsp cumin seeds

1 large onion, chopped

1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste

1 cup tomato puree

1 tsp turmeric powder

1 tsp coriander powder

1 tsp cumin powder

1 tsp garam masala

1/2 tsp red chili powder

Salt to taste

Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

tools

Pressure Cooker (if using dried chickpeas)

Heavy Bottom Pan

Measuring Spoons

Wooden Spoon
